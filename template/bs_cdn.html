Apachi 310RR

The TVS Apache RR 310 is a touring bike made by the TVS Motor Company in collaboration with BMW Motorrad that was launched in India on December 6, 2017. it uses a 312.2cc single-cylinder, four-stroke, SI, liquid-cooled, DOHC reverse inclined engine.
